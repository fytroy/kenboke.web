Our Story

Built on a foundation of reliability and a passion for the open road.

Kenbo Kenya is the official home of Kenbo motorcycles in Kenya. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, durable motorcycles that are perfectly suited for the diverse landscape and needs of Kenyan riders.

Our catalog features a range of models, including efficient electric bikes and powerful petrol motorcycles. We believe in providing options that cater to everyone, whether you're a daily commuter or an adventurous explorer.

Since August 2025, we have been committed to delivering not just motorcycles, but a promise of reliability and performance. Our bikes are a testament to our mission: to make every ride in Kenya an exceptional experience.
